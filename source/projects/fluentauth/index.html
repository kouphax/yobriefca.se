Tinyweb.FluentAuth

Authentication for Tinyweb with a simple Fluent syntax.... What the hell did you expect?

Install-Package Tinyweb.FluentAuth

Simple, Single-Point Configuration

Don't suffer from arbitrary attribute or method pollution across handlers! FluentSecutiry gives you a single point of configuration and offers a simple yet powerful syntax for tailoring your web authentication needs.

Simple, Fluent Syntax

Don't be bogged down by big fancy nomenclature or long winded method names ever again! Use a simple, straight to the point syntax to configure all your authentication needs without all the high ceremony vocabulary. It aint rocket science biatch!


Complex and Conditional Rules

Got a part of your web app that needs a random collection of request headers, roles and http verbs? You bet your ass we got your ass covered, you betcha, yeah. Build a set of complex rules around handlers that are executed conditionally on a "per-request" basis and watch the awesome unfold!

Built Against ASP.NET

Tinyweb was built on top of ASP.NET to give you access to the great features already available. FluentAuth is no different. Use any membership provider or custom IPrincipal you want. We don't care, you're the boss and we'll take whatever you can give us - more or less!
